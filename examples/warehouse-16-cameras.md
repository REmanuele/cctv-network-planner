# Example: Warehouse - 16 Cameras

## Scenario

- 16 IP cameras
- 4 Mbps per camera
- 24/7 recording
- 20 days retention
- 10 W PoE consumption per camera

## Estimated result

- Total bandwidth: about 65 Mbps
- Estimated storage: about 13 TB
- Recommended disk size: 16 TB minimum
- Estimated PoE budget: about 192 W including safety margin

## Technical considerations

For this type of system, it is important to check:

- NVR incoming bandwidth;
- switch uplink capacity;
- PoE budget;
- recording retention;
- future expansion margin.
