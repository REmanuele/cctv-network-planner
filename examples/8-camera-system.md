# Example: 8-camera CCTV system

## Input

- 8 IP cameras
- 4 Mbps per camera
- 24/7 recording
- 15 days retention
- 8 W PoE per camera

## Estimated result

- Total bandwidth: about 32 Mbps
- Estimated storage: about 5.2 TB
- Recommended disk size: 6 TB minimum
- Estimated PoE budget with margin: about 77 W
