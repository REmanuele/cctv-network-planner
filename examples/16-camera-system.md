# Example: 16-camera CCTV system

## Input

- 16 IP cameras
- 3 Mbps per camera
- 24/7 recording
- 30 days retention
- 7 W PoE per camera

## Estimated result

- Total bandwidth: about 48 Mbps
- Estimated storage: about 15.5 TB
- Recommended disk size: 16 TB minimum
- Estimated PoE budget with margin: about 135 W
