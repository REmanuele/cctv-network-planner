# PoE Budget Example

## Scenario

- 8 cameras
- 8 W average consumption per camera
- 20% safety margin

## Calculation

Base consumption:

```text
8 cameras x 8 W = 64 W
```

With safety margin:

```text
64 W x 1.2 = 76.8 W
```

## Result

The PoE switch should provide at least 76.8 W total PoE budget.

In a real installation, always check:

- maximum PoE budget per port;
- total switch PoE budget;
- cable length;
- camera IR consumption at night;
- environmental conditions.
