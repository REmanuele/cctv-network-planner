# Example: Small Shop - 4 Cameras

## Scenario

- 4 IP cameras
- 4 Mbps per camera
- 24/7 recording
- 15 days retention
- 8 W PoE consumption per camera

## Estimated result

- Total bandwidth: about 16 Mbps
- Estimated storage: about 2.6 TB
- Recommended disk size: 3 TB minimum
- Estimated PoE budget: about 38 W including safety margin

## Notes

This is a simplified planning example. Final sizing depends on codec, scene complexity, recording mode and manufacturer-specific behavior.
